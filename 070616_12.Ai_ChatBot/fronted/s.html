Good Samaritan Law

Save lives without fear. The law protects bystanders who help road accident victims.

Why This Law Matters

Thousands of lives are lost in road accidents every year because victims do not get timely help. The Good Samaritan Law ensures that any person who helps accident victims is protected from legal and procedural hassles.

Your Rights Under the Law

Legal Protection

Helpers are not liable for injury or death of victims while providing assistance in good faith.

No Harassment

Police and hospitals cannot force you to reveal identity or stay against your will.

Free & Voluntary

You can choose to stay anonymous and are not bound to pay hospital costs.

  • Provide first aid if trained.
